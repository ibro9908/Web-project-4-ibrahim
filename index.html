Residential

Naawi-Oodena’s residential spaces are thoughtfully designed to blend modern living with cultural heritage. These homes aim to foster a strong sense of community, where families can grow together in a safe, inclusive, and vibrant environment. The neighborhood includes a mix of single-family homes, townhouses, and apartments — all built with sustainable materials and respectful of the land. Walking paths, green spaces, and gathering areas create opportunities for neighbors to connect and celebrate traditions. Future residents can look forward to a lifestyle that combines comfort, nature, and cultural pride — right at the heart of a growing community.


Commercial /Mixed-Use

Commercial Naawi-Oodena’s commercial spaces are designed to support thriving local businesses while reflecting the community's cultural values. With room for retail shops, restaurants, and markets, the area invites residents and visitors alike to connect, support entrepreneurship, and experience authentic offerings in a modern yet respectful setting. The mixed-use developments at Naawi-Oodena blend residential, retail, and community spaces into walkable, vibrant neighborhoods. These spaces aim to promote convenience, sustainability, and a strong sense of community, all while maintaining a close connection to the land and its people.


Community Spaces

Naawi-Oodena’s community spaces are places of gathering, ceremony, and shared experience. Parks, green areas, and cultural centers are thoughtfully designed to bring people together for events, storytelling, and celebrations that honor Indigenous traditions while welcoming all. These spaces reflect unity, resilience, and a deep respect for the land.


Sports and Recreation

The sports and recreation facilities at Naawi-Oodena offer opportunities for wellness, teamwork, and active living. From multi-use fields and basketball courts to indoor arenas and walking trails, these spaces encourage all generations to engage in physical activity, build community spirit, and celebrate health through movement rooted in balance and tradition.


Cultural Campus + Education

The Cultural Campus and education spaces at Naawi-Oodena are centers for learning, language, and legacy. These spaces honor Indigenous knowledge systems while integrating modern education. With schools, libraries, and cultural centers, the community empowers youth and lifelong learners through teachings that reflect identity, history, and a shared future.


Mixed-use village

The Mixed-Use Village at Naawi-Oodena brings together homes, shops, services, and workplaces in one vibrant hub. Designed for walkability and community connection, this area blends traditional values with modern living—creating a place where people can live, work, and gather all in one space.
